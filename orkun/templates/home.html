About Me

I am a R&D Engineer with a strong background in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. My expertise includes circuit design, embedded system software development, and product development. I am proficient in a wide range of programming languages, tools, and platforms, including C, C++, Python, Visual Studio Code, Keil, STM32CubeIDE and Altium Designer. With problem-solving and analytical skills, I have successfully delivered multiple projects.

Moreover, I have demonstrated leadership skills by leading and mentoring various teams, including the Robotics and Automation Community in IEEE, and the ATAY Team, where I played a key role in designing and manufacturing electric vehicles. I am a quick learner, always eager to take on new challenges, and passionate about staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends. With communication skills, I am capable of working efficiently and effectively in a team environment while also delivering results independently.
